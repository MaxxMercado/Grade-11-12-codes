ATTACK TO WATCH OUT FOR

Savage Slams and Sweeps


Radahn will sometimes attack with semi random sweeps and slams. Most of the time these will be isolated, but he can chain them together up to (typically) a chain of 4.


HOW TO DODGE

There are two main ways to dodge the attack, the method depends on the distance between you and her:


These attacks are slow and predictable. Dodge them in isolation. If he chains 2 slams together, it means he will chain 2 more after. If you notice this, dodge towards him. You are safe under him until he finishes the chain. Get a few free hits in and dodge away when his chain is done.
