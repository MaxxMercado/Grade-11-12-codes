"The Golden Order "

Astel, Naturalborn of the Void is a strange alien lifeform comprised of many-colored star debris which landed in the Lands Between long ago, and is found in Grand Cloister, Lake of Rot. Astel destroyed one of the Eternal Cities and wiped out their sky. Seeing as how both the Eternal Cities of Nokron and Nokstella still have their starry night sky, it is possible that the Nameless Eternal City that the Site of Grace in Deeproot Depths was named after was Astel's unfortunate target.

ATTACK TO WATCH OUT FOR

Waves of Darkness


An attack telegraphed by three glowing orbs on its pairs of hands. It will slam it down and create shockwave that repeats itself three times, with a higher radius each time


HOW TO DODGE

Run away from it or block to reduce damage. The "pulse" is too quick for Medium and Heavy builds to dodge.


1)When the target is in mid to long-range, it will attack them with their tail, slamming it down twice.

2) Dodge left or right. Getting close enough will avoid this attack completely.

3) Highly telegraphed, you can hit its head while it is busy doing this move.

4)Astel positions its tail above its head, before striking the ground 3 times with the barb on its tail.
