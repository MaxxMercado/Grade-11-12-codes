Legends
Juicewrld

“Legends” is a tribute song to late rappers XXXTENTACION and Lil Peep.
The song was made in response to the passing of XXXTENTACION on
June 18, 2018. Juice WRLD previewed this song on his Instagram live the
same day he dropped it. As shown by the timeline of events, Juice created
this entire song in just under a day.

Produced by
Russ Chell & Take a Daytrip
Release Date June 19, 2018
