The New Balance 550 White Green features a smooth white leather upper
with a grey suede toe wrap and green detailing. From there, a New Balance
Basketball woven tongue label and matching sole completes the design.
Re-introducing a basketball legend. The return of the 550 pays tribute
to the 1989 original that defined a hoops generation. Originally worn by
pro ballers in the ’80s and ’90s, the new 550 is simple, clean and true to its
legacy. This kid-friendly version delivers mini-me style.
